Frequently Asked Questions

What laparoscopic surgeries does Dr. Vishal Diddi perform?

Dr. Vishal specializes in laparoscopic hernia repair, gallbladder removal, weight loss (bariatric) surgery, and gastrointestinal procedures.

Is laparoscopic surgery better than open surgery?

Yes, laparoscopic surgery is a minimally invasive technique with smaller incisions, less pain, faster recovery, and reduced risk of complications compared to open surgery.

What is the recovery time after laser surgery for piles or hernia?

Most patients recover within a few days after laser treatment for piles and within a week after laparoscopic hernia repair, depending on individual healing rates.

Does Dr. Vishal Diddi offer post-surgery care and follow-ups?

Yes, he provides comprehensive post-surgical care, follow-ups, and personalized recovery guidance to ensure the best outcomes.

Does Dr. Vishal Diddi accept insurance for surgeries?

Yes, most health insurance plans cover laparoscopic and laser surgeries. It is recommended to check with your insurance provider or the clinic for coverage details.

What are the signs that I might need hernia surgery?

You should consult Dr. Vishal if you experience persistent pain, a visible bulge, discomfort during movement, or digestive issues related to a hernia.
